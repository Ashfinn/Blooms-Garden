About Us

Bringing nature's beauty to your doorstep

Based in beautiful Tucson, Arizona, our small but dedicated team specializes in garden design, maintenance, and plant sales. We believe that a well-designed garden can bring beauty and tranquility to your life, and we're here to make that a reality.

Whether you're looking for a stunning garden makeover, need regular maintenance, or want to bring the outdoors in with our handpicked selection of plants, Lucky Shrub has you covered. Our founder, Jason, is a garden design maestro, and Maria takes care of our thriving plant nursery. Together, they've cultivated a business rooted in their love for plants and a desire to make your outdoor space flourish.
